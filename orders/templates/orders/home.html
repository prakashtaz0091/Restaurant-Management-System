OUR STORY

A teahouse, grown into a kitchen.

ChaiAdda began as a single table and a kettle of chiya — a place for neighbours to sit, talk, and eat well. That spirit hasn't changed. What's grown is the kitchen behind it: a chef-led team dedicated to preparing traditional Nepali dishes with the same care you'd find in a home kitchen, at the pace a real meal deserves.

  • 01 Recipes passed down rather than reinvented
  • 02 Spices ground fresh, in small batches, daily
  • 03 A kitchen that cooks to order, never in advance
  • 04 A dining room built for long, unhurried meals
